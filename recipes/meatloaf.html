Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a great recipe that the whole family will enjoy! It is simple to make and quick to do!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • ketchup
  • bread crumbs
  • parmesean cheese
  • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, ketchup, BBQ sauce, parmesean cheese, and bread crumbs.
  3. Mix well and shape into loaf.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for one hour.
